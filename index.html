"Spirited Away" is a celebrated animated film by Hayao Miyazaki, featuring a young girl named Chihiro who navigates a magical, enigmatic world to rescue her parents. Its rich visuals, imaginative storytelling, and emotional depth have earned it critical acclaim and widespread admiration.
